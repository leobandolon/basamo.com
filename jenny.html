Jenny-the-Gennie
Written by Shayne Gian A. Layague

Marialyn lives with Lola Sita and Lolo Paquito in a small town near the sea, where the villagers catch fish for a living. Ever since her parents died when she was six years old due to drowning in the sea during a storm surge, Marialyn has developed a fear of the sea, or thalassophobia. She hates the ocean because she remembers how it devoured her parents, never to see them again.

One day, her Lola Sita and Lolo Paquito arrived with a sad look on their faces.

"Did something bad happen, Lolo?" she asked.

“We did not have enough catch today because the fish are quite few," Lolo Paquito responded.

Marialyn sighed and said “If only we could grow vegetables and fruits in our backyard, we would not have to struggle catching and selling fish."

"You know how hard it is to plant vegetables or fruit in our town. Our soil is not fit for that," Lola Sita objected.

"We can only pray for a miracle if that’s the case."

Lola Sita and Lolo Paquito prepared their dinner for the night while listening to the small radio they have.

"There will be an upcoming storm that will hit the area; all are advised to stay inside their houses and prepare for the storm," the announcer said.
