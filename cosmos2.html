“Cosmos Love Blooms”
By: Marielle Stella C. Pandanduyan

The two insects spent their days exploring the garden of Eco-Zone together, sharing stories, and reveling in the beauty of their surroundings. Jakemar's heart fluttered like his wings whenever he was near Lady Faith, and Lady Faith found herself charmed by Jakemar's genuine spirit.

As the seasons changed, so did their relationship. The garden of Eco-Zone became a canvas of colors, echoing the vibrant love between Jakemar and Lady Faith. They danced among the blossoms; their hearts intertwined like vines on a trellis.

One day, as the sun set over the garden, Jakemar and Lady Faith perched on a cosmos petal, looking out over the expanse of their world.

"Jakemar," Lady Faith said, "I never thought a caterpillar and a ladybug could find such deep connection and love."

"Love knows no boundaries," Jakemar replied, his antennae twitching with joy. "Just like our garden, our love is a beautiful and ever-growing tapestry."

And so, under the moonlit sky, Jakemar and Lady Faith continued their enchanting journey, their love story becoming a cherished tale whispered among the flowers, reminding all who heard it that love can bloom in the most unexpected places, transcending the boundaries of form and color.
