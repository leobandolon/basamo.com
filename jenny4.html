Jenny-the-Gennie
Written by Shayne Gian A. Layague

“I wish for our land to become as fertile as it ever was. I wish for it to be suitable for planting trees, crops, vegetables, and fruits. In that way, people won’t be tied to the idea that we were born to risk our lives every time we sail out into the ocean."

Jenny the Genie clapped her hands and exclaimed,

"Your wish is my command!"

In a blink of an eye, trees sprouted everywhere. Vegetables, fruits, and crops are now seen growing rapidly. Everyone in town shouted for joy; they finally have other livelihoods to be busy with.

"Now, for my last wish. I wish for my grandparents to be safe. They were lost since last night, and I cannot find them. I’m scared to be alone for the rest of my life."

Jenny the Genie smiled and said,

"They are safe and sound; they’ve been looking for you as well."

Marialyn smiled and asked Jenny,

"If you were to be given a wish, what would you wish for?"

Jenny the Genie looked at her and said,

“Freedom—I've been imprisoned inside the jar ever since I can remember. I cannot even recall my origin nor my family."
