Jenny-the-Gennie
Written by Shayne Gian A. Layague

“For my last wish, then, Jenny the Genie, I wish for your freedom and for you to become part of my family. You can be my little sister!"

Jenny was shocked at her final wish, but everything went as smoothly as it could. Her freedom was granted; she is no longer Jennie the Genie but Marialyn’s little sister. She wanted to become normal like everyone else, and somebody granted it to her.

Marialyn and Jennie went back to their home, and there they saw Lolo Paquito and Lola Sita waiting for them. With a big smile on their faces, they rushed towards each other.

Marialyn! It looked like a miracle happened! Remember what you said last night? Our soil is now suitable for planting crops! We can now be farmers! And oohhhh, who’s this little girl? Lola Sita asked.

"She’s my little sister, and her name is Jenny."

"Jenny, what a nice name you have! Welcome to our home. It’s not much, but we will give you all the love you need."

Jenny cried and smiled.

"Yes, I am home. Finally, I am home."

Marialyn and Jenny lived together with their grandparents. Planting and growing crops for a livelihood and sometimes going out into the ocean It was just like what everybody wished for—a town with a peaceful life and a calm ocean.
