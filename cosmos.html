“Cosmos Love Blooms”
By: Marielle Stella C. Pandanduyan

In the heart of a lush garden of Eco-Zone, lived a young and vibrant caterpillar named Jakemar. With his green and yellow stripes, he spent his days munching on leaves and exploring the world around him. One sunny day, while traversing the delicate petals of a cosmos, Jakemar laid eyes on the most enchanting ladybug he had ever seen.

Her name was Lady Faith, a ladybug with wings painted in the brightest shades of red and black. She gracefully moved from flower to flower, sipping nectar and spreading her charm. Jakemar was captivated by her beauty and her delicate, dotted wings.

Determined to get closer to Lady Faith, Jakemar began to transform. He spun a silken cocoon around himself, patiently waiting for the day he would emerge as a butterfly. Days turned into weeks, and Jakemar's metamorphosis neared completion. As he burst forth from his cocoon, he revealed wings adorned with the most exquisite patterns of greens and blues.

With newfound elegance, Jakemar took to the skies, fluttering toward Lady Faith. The ladybug, perched on a sunflower, looked up in surprise as Jakemar approached.

"Hello, Lady Faith. It's me, Jakemar," he said, his wings shimmering in the sunlight.

Lady Faith, with a flutter of her own wings, blushed. "Oh, Jakemar, you've transformed into something truly breathtaking.
